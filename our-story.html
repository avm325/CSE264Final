Our Orgins


Banaza is a yearly food festial that began in 2018. It is held in the historic Pike Place Market and supported by some of Seattle's finest buisness'. We are proud to showcase cuisine from around the world, cooked by lovely people that hail from near and far. You'll find some of the best and freshest seafood , the most punchy coffee, and some of the grooviest tunes if you join us.

Our Commitment


What sets Bananza apart is its commitment to responsible sourcing and culinary excellence. Every ingredient is carefully selected, ensuring the highest quality and freshness, while each dish is cooked to perfection, delighting the senses with every mouthful. Moreover, Bananza goes beyond just serving delicious food; it also embodies a spirit of community and generosity. All extra food and ingredients are graciously donated to local soup kitchens in Seattle, making a meaningful impact on those in need. Come join us at Bananza and embark on a culinary journey like no other, where every bite tells a story and every meal makes a difference.
