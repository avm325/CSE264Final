FAQ



What is your refund policy?

Unfortunately, our tickets are nonrefundable.
Our chefs and vendors have traveled far and wide to help showcase their skills and ingredients! Our best suggestion is to mark your calendars or gift the tickets to a friend!

What is your exchange policy?

Our tickets are non-exchangeable. However, if you have extenuating circumstances, please contact our customer support team, and we'll do our best to assist you.

What time do the gates open/close each day?

Our opening times are 11am - 10pm on the festival days! Wear conmfy shoes and come hungry.

Can I bring a child to the festival?

Bananza guests must be ages 18+. This one's for the parents, sorry kiddo's!
